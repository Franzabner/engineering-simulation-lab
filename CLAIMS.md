# Claims

Status: scaffolded

## Allowed Claims

- This is a local-only scaffold for public-safe engineering simulation and validation studies.
- The repo uses synthetic inputs and documentation templates.
- The repo documents assumptions, model limits, validation questions, and what a study does not prove.

## Forbidden Claims

- Do not claim private traces, private measurements, production thermal data, production airflow data, production power data, or private workloads are included.
- Do not claim benchmark validation, physical validation, deployment, certification, product readiness, released models, released datasets, Spaces, or proof completion.
- Do not imply customer data, Foundation-private data, internal company product names, or sealed assumptions are public.
